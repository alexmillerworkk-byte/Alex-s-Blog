## Oceanfront Luxury: Beach Wedding Venues You’ll Adore

The country’s Pacific coast is home to some of the most breathtaking beach wedding venues in Costa Rica — ideal for seaside vows.

**Vista Hermosa Estate (Manuel Antonio):** An exclusive villa with a wide overview of the ocean and elegant tropical architecture. It is ideal for destination weddings in Costa Rica because of its classy rooms and green environment.  

**Villa Puntel de Vista (Manuel Antonio):** The villa is a luxurious multi-storey structure that combines modern construction and magnificent ocean views. It is among the best [Costa Rica wedding](https://mapachetours.com/guide-topic/wedding-in-costa-rica) villas to have exclusive wedding celebrations.  

**Zephyr Palace (Jaco):** A beautiful palace on a hill surrounded by the Pacific Ocean, which is perfect during romantic sunset ceremonies. The rich interiors and the views make it a fairytale experience.  

**Casa Chameleon in Las Catalinas:** An adults-only resort where one experiences closeness, ocean winds, and a relaxing romantic atmosphere. Couples prefer it because of its privacy and spectacular sea-view wedding venues.

## Romantic Resort Venues Along Costa Rica’s Pacific Coast

If you dream of a lavish celebration, you’ll love these luxury wedding villas Costa Rica offers along the coast.

**Four Seasons Resort at Peninsula Papagayo:** A five-star resort with five-star services and with oceanfront views. Vows can be exchanged by the couples in beautiful Pacific backgrounds and experience the best amenities in the world.  

**The Westin Reserva Conchal:** This is an all-inclusive beach resort which is encircled by white sands and tropical gardens. It is a combination of both relaxation and sophistication with services ranging to a spa experience to fancy parties.  

**Andaz Costa Rica Resort:** This is a modern resort which is a perfect blend of Costa Rican culture and modern design. The terraces with the ocean view and its colorful ambience are the ideal background of a romantic vow.  

**Hacienda Pinilla (Tamarindo):** It is a casual-upscale venue with beach villas, outdoor spaces and natural beauty. It is ideal when it comes to small groups that enjoy golden beaches and tropical scenery.

## Enchanting Jungle, Mountain & Waterfall Wedding Venues

For nature-loving couples, rainforest wedding venues in Costa Rica are pure magic.

**The Springs Resort and Spa (Arenal):** A jungle paradise that is engulfed with green rain forests and spectacular views of the volcanoes. Couples are able to enjoy love either in natural hot springs or in fancy garden terraces.  

**Hotel Arenal Kioro Suites & Spa:** This hotel is located in the middle of greenery and has the view of Arenal Volcano and calm gardens. The venue is a combination of comfort, nature and luxury in an experience that is memorable in terms of wedding.  

**Nauyaca Waterfall (Dominical):** Nauyaca Waterfall is a secret paradise where you will be able to make any commitments right before the great falls. It is a perfect place to be in when one wants to be in a nature that is raw and untouched.

## Intimate & Offbeat Wedding Venues for a Private Celebration

Couples looking for quiet, exclusive spots will find plenty of private wedding villas in Costa Rica that feel personal and peaceful.

**Nosara Beach Hotel (Nosara):** It is a beautiful boutique hotel ideal for barefoot beach weddings and golden sunsets. It provides a relaxed environment in the presence of tropical nature and the ocean air.  

**Las Catalinas:** A beautiful vacation resort that bears a romantic getaway that is movie-like. The cobblestone streets, seaside views, and small spaces make it ideal in eloping.  

**Elite Beach Villas (Playa Hermosa):** Exclusive luxury villas with spectacular oceanic view and complete privacy. Both villas offer a unique, luxurious ambiance of a small intimate party or a renewal of vows.
